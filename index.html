Warning Signs Your Edmond
Foundation Needs Attention Now

Sticking Doors & Windows

Sudden sticking or misalignment in doors and windows is often the first visible sign of foundation movement in Edmond homes.

Cracks in Walls or Floors

Diagonal cracks near door frames or stair-step cracks in brick wider than 1/4 inch need professional evaluation right away.

Sloping or Uneven Floors

Floors that slope or feel bouncy indicate settling beneath — extremely common in Edmond's newer subdivisions on clay soil.

Gaps Around Exterior

Gaps between siding, trim, or brickwork and the structure indicate your foundation is pulling away and needs immediate attention.
